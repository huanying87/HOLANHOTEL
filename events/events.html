Events

EVENTS MOUUU

As the sun begins its descent into the horizon, signaling the arrival of balmy summer evenings, the residents of our coastal town eagerly await a quartet of thrilling events that promise to make this summer one for the books. From the competitive Sand Tennis Ball tournament to an electrifying Electronica Concert, a cozy Bonfire Party, and a sun-soaked Beach Sports Competition, there's something for everyone in this summer extravaganza. So, get ready to mark your calendars and dive into the sizzling action!

    Sand Tennis Ball Tournament

  • Date: July 15th - July 17th
  • Location: Holan Beachside Tennis Courts

Prepare to witness some of the fiercest tennis action on the sandy courts of our picturesque beach. The Sand Tennis Ball Tournament is back, attracting tennis enthusiasts from all over the region. With the breathtaking backdrop of the sun-kissed shoreline, competitors will battle it out in a thrilling display of agility and finesse. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a casual tennis fan, this event promises top-notch entertainment and a fantastic atmosphere.



    Electronica Concert

  • Date: July 22nd
  • Location: Holan Beachfront Amphitheater

The electrifying beats of the Electronica Concert will reverberate through the night air, transforming the beachfront amphitheater into a dancefloor under the stars. Renowned DJs and electronic music artists are set to take the stage, promising an unforgettable night of pulsating rhythms and stunning visuals. So, put on your dancing shoes and get ready to groove to the tunes of summer!

    Bonfire Party

  • Date: August 5th
  • Location:Holan Beach Bonfire Pit

Gather 'round the crackling flames as we celebrate the summer spirit with our annual Bonfire Party. This cozy and communal event is perfect for families and friends to come together, roast marshmallows, share stories, and stargaze. Don't forget your acoustic instruments for an impromptu beachside jam session. It's a night of warmth, camaraderie, and memories waiting to be made.


    Sport on the Beach Competition

  • Date: August 19th - August 20th
  • Location: Holan Beach

For those who prefer action in the sun and surf, the Sport on the Beach Competition is the event to watchâ€”or better yet, participate in! Teams will compete in various beach sports like beach volleyball, ultimate frisbee, paddleball, and more. With the ocean waves as the backdrop, this competition promises thrills, spills, and loads of beachside fun.


    The Milestone Birthday Bash

  • Event Date: October 5th, 2023
  • birthday theme: The Lakeside Pavilion

Turning fifty never looked so good! Mark, a beloved member of our community, celebrated his milestone birthday in style at The Lakeside Pavilion. Friends and family gathered to honor Mark's life, filled with achievements and cherished memories.

The pavilion was transformed into a vibrant oasis, complete with colorful decorations and a photo booth for capturing the fun moments. Mark's love for live music was celebrated with a local band playing his favorite tunes. A highlight of the evening was Mark's heartfelt speech, thanking everyone for their presence and support throughout the years.


    The Grand Wedding Affair

  • Event Date: September 15th, 2023
  • Wedding Theme: The Crystal Gardens

The sun kissed the horizon with hues of orange and pink as Sarah and Michael exchanged their vows in a heartwarming ceremony at The Crystal Gardens. The venue's lush gardens and sparkling crystal chandeliers provided the perfect backdrop for their fairy-tale wedding.

The bride's flowing gown and the groom's sharp tuxedo were complemented by the smiles of their friends and family. Tears of joy and laughter filled the air as they sealed their love with a kiss. The reception that followed was nothing short of magical, with a decadent feast, a live band, and dancing under the stars.

This summer promises an array of exciting events that cater to all tastes and preferences. Whether you're into sports, music, or simply enjoying the serene beauty of the beach, these events offer the perfect opportunity to embrace the spirit of summer. So, gather your friends and family, and make this summer a memorable one by joining us for the Sand Tennis Ball Tournament, the Electronica Concert, the Bonfire Party, and the Sport on the Beach Competition. Let the good times roll as we soak up the sun and create lasting memories on our beautiful coastal shores!
